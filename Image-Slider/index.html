Sigiriya
Climb the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya, a marvel of engineering with its elaborate gardens, frescoes, and panoramic views from the summit.
Galle
Wander through the charming streets of Galle, a historic fort city with Dutch colonial architecture, cobblestone streets, and beautiful ocean views.
Ella
Admire the breathtaking landscapes of Ella, known for its stunning viewpoints, tea plantations, and the famous Nine Arches Bridge.
Kandy
Visit Kandy, the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic and surrounded by lush green hills and a picturesque lake.
Mirissa
Relax on the pristine beaches of Mirissa, famous for whale watching, surfing, and enjoying the serene beauty of the Indian Ocean.
Anuradhapura
Discover the ancient city of Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its well-preserved ruins of Sri Lankan civilization and sacred Buddhist sites.
Polonnaruwa
Explore Polonnaruwa, a historic city with stunning archaeological relics, including royal palaces, temples, and the iconic Gal Vihara stone statues.
Pinnawala
Get up close with elephants at the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage, where you can watch these gentle giants being fed and bathed.
Adam's Peak
Embark on a pilgrimage to Adam's Peak, a sacred mountain with a footprint at its summit, revered by multiple religions and offering spectacular sunrise views.
Colombo
Experience the vibrant city life of Colombo, where modern skyscrapers blend with colonial architecture, bustling markets, and a scenic coastline.
